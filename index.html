Who Are We?

OPPORTUNA FOUNDATION LIMITED is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to advancing the well-being of marginalized communities in Uganda. Our multifaceted approach focuses on various essential aspects, including child education, livelihood programs, support for the old and children living with disabilities, combating issues such as child marriages and teenage pregnancy, and empowering both youth and women in communities.

Located in Kampala

Learn More

Our Mission

To improve the lives of many marginalized community members with much emphasis on children, women, and youth through humanitarian activities and educational programs, among other free services.

Cores and Values

Empowerment: The organization aims to empower marginalized community members, especially children, women, and youth, by providing them with resources, opportunities, and support to improve their lives and achieve their full potential.

Nourishing Lives: A Beneficiary of our Food Assistance Program

Anna, a single mother of three, faced financial hardships that made it difficult to put food on the table for her family. Through our initiatives, we provided her with essential food packs, ensuring her children have access to nutritious meals.

Empowering Smiles: The Impact of Clothing Assistance Program

Meet a bright and energetic young girl, whose life has been touched by the clothing assistance program at Opportuna Foundation Limited. Her name remains anonymous, but her smile speaks volumes.
